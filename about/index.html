About the Home

A warm home where every life is honored.

We believe in the sanctity of life and are committed to honoring the dignity and worth of every individual we serve.

Our philosophy is simple: treat each resident with the same kindness, respect, and quality of care that we would want for ourselves and our loved ones.

Life Is Worth Living AFH LLC is owned and operated by Zekariase and Alem, who bring decades of healthcare experience and a deep passion for serving others.

Meet the Owners

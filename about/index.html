About Us

A home founded on clinical excellence and genuine compassion.

Life Worth Living was created to give seniors professional oversight in a peaceful, personal setting.

Our Story

This Adult Family Home was founded and operated by a Registered Nurse with 15+ years of experience in long-term care, memory care, rehabilitation, and hospital nursing. After years of serving seniors in clinical settings, she created a home where residents receive professional medical oversight combined with warm, family-centered care.

Mission

To provide exceptional, dignified, and person-centered care in a safe, loving home environment.

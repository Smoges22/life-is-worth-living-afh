Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Life Is Worth Living Adult Family Home, LLC respects your privacy. This website collects only the information you choose to provide through contact or tour request forms, such as your name, phone number, email address, preferred tour date, and message.

How Information Is Used

Information submitted through this website is used to respond to inquiries, coordinate tours, and communicate about potential care needs. We do not sell personal information.

Health Information

Please avoid submitting detailed medical information through website forms. Sensitive care details should be discussed directly by phone or during an appropriate consultation.
